Kurupira

Missão

HERMES is an innovative radio solution crafted to bridge the communication gap for isolated and endangered communities. Our mission is to empower these communities by providing them with access to vital information, emergency alerts, and educational resources. By fostering reliable communication channels, Kurupira aims to enhance community resilience, promote safety, and support sustainable development.
